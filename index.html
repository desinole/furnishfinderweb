Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about renting in Chattahoochee, FL

What types of leases do you offer?

We offer both monthly and annual lease options on our rental properties. The lease type available varies by property. Contact us for specific terms and pricing on any listing.

How do I find out pricing for a property?

Click the "Contact for Pricing" button on any property listing. Fill out the inquiry form and we'll get back to you promptly with pricing details, availability, and lease terms.

Are there pet-friendly rentals available?

Yes! Several of our properties are pet-friendly. Look for the pet-friendly badge on listings. Properties with fenced yards are especially great for dog owners. Contact us for specific pet policies on any listing.

Do you offer furnished rentals?

Some of our properties are fully furnished and move-in ready, while others are unfurnished. Furnished properties are marked with a "Furnished" badge and include essentials like furniture, kitchen supplies, linens, and WiFi.

How far is Chattahoochee from nearby cities?

Chattahoochee FL is centrally located in the Florida Panhandle:

  • Sneads, FL: 10 minutes
  • Quincy, FL: 20 minutes
  • Marianna, FL: 25 minutes
  • Donalsonville, GA: 25 minutes
  • Bainbridge, GA: 30 minutes
  • Tallahassee, FL: 45 minutes
Is Chattahoochee good for travel nurses?

Absolutely. Florida State Hospital is located right in Chattahoochee. You're also within commuting distance of Jackson Hospital (Marianna, 25 min) and multiple Tallahassee hospitals (45 min). Several of our properties cater to healthcare professionals with flexible lease terms.

What outdoor activities are nearby?

Chattahoochee is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts:

  • Lake Seminole: World-class bass fishing, boating, water sports
  • Apalachicola River: Fishing, kayaking, canoeing
  • Three Rivers State Park: Hiking trails, camping, wildlife viewing
